About Me

With a strong foundation in computational physics and engineering, I bring a wealth of expertise in developing, validating, and applying mathematical models to complex technical challenges. My experience spans across various programming languages and software tools, including Python and C++, making me well-equipped to tackle diverse software development projects. In my career, I have created both computational physics models and data processing pipelines, and I have also executed several data analysis and machine learning projects.

Additionally, I have served as a project manager and in this capacity, I have planned, delegated, and overseen the implementation of several computational models and software tools. This blend of technical proficiency and project management experience positions me well for the challenges and responsibilities of freelance expert technical work.

My academic credentials include a PhD from the Institute of Physics of the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne and a Joint Master in Nuclear Engineering from Swiss Federal Institutes of Technology in Zurich and Lausanne. These experiences have honed my analytical skills and my ability to translate complex data into actionable insights.
